Pokemon Gen 1 Starters

A Brief Summary of the Kanto Region Starters

There are 8 regions in the world of Pokemon with each having 3 starter Pokemon native to the region. The first 2 regions contain:

  1. Kanto
    • Bulbasaur
    • Charmander
    • Squirtle
  2. Johto
    • Chikorita
    • Cyndaquil
    • Totodile
